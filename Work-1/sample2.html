Congratulations to Our Learner
 
 
Bijit Deka
Data Science Analyst @WeWork

Meet Bijit Deka, a GreyAtom learner who successfully pivoted his career to Data Science in spite of coming from a different educational  background. He started off as a mechanical engineer, who contributed as a content writer.

Always strong mathematically, Bijit initially struggled with coding. But he had grit like no other. He would make mistakes like everyone else, and every time that happened, he got up and reattempted the coding tasks.

It was his perservance to learn that helped him upskill and bridge the gap between data science and his educational background.

Bijit is now a valued Data Science Analyst at WeWork, Bengaluru, working with US clients and Team GreyAtom couldn't be more proud of him.

Learner Tribute
 
Prerna Singhal
Cohort: DSMR 20th Jan 2019

"In my learning journey with GreyAtom, in addition to the constant help from my mentors, an important part has been attempting projects that are provided as a part of the curriculum. There are hands-on tasks and code-along practice sessions included. The experience of doing real projects has been absolutely critical to my learning and pivoting my career. While projects are important to gain technical skills, an understanding of the different job roles available is very important.

GreyAtom has been great with their support with projects, scheduling career services sessions and sending me constant reminders to keep me on track. Do not miss mock personal interviews since the feedback received from mentors is invaluable to cracking interviews."
